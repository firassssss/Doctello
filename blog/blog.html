Healthy Eating Habits

Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains. Avoid processed foods and excessive sugar intake.

Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water throughout the day. Limit consumption of sugary drinks and alcohol.

Managing Stress Levels

Practice relaxation techniques such as deep breathing, meditation, or yoga to reduce stress and promote mental well-being.

Make time for hobbies and activities you enjoy to help relieve stress and improve overall mood.
